Problem or opportunity

Uganda like other african countries are still lagging behind in technology, because there are few skilled personel in the information technology sector. Most of the work is being done manually hence incurring alot of costs interms of money and time

Fecundlabs is looking for looking for finance to help out in buying machines paying for internet, buying server space and paying our rental and taxes.

Solution

We intend to develop a technology center with free internet where people can come present their proposed ideas, get conselling and when approved by the team they can use our facilities for their software development purpose.

Why now

If Uganda is to develop Information technology should be improved, by helping those with innovative ideas to get facilitation.
