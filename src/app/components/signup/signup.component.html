Why ImukaAccess?

Deal Origination

Source deals from previously hard-to-reach areas. ImukaAccess gives you an immediate connection to the most creative and innovative entrepreneurs in East Africa. We source businesses on the forefront of social and economic growth and link them to you.

Business Growth Visibility

The ImukaAccess platform lets you watch businesses' progress in real time, both before and after you make the decision to invest. We validate, track and share businesses' momentum and key milestones so that you can make the most informed decisions.

On-ground Validation support

At ImukaAccess we invest in thoroughly trained and experienced expert mentors who directly interact with entrepreneurs and assess businesses for validity of services and structure and growth-readiness. The due diligence process is essential to our support model, saving you time and costs, and reducing investment risks for both investor and entrepreneur.

Investment Portfolio

Africa is Rising: it's time to grow and diversify your investment portfolio. Be at the centre of economic growth in regions with the natural resources, human capital and sheer determination for social innovation. We offer access to a broad range of businesses with creative solutions in every sector.

Clear Exit Opportunity

Seed, startup, growth or expansion? We know that each investor has unique preferences for the stage at which they invest in businesses. We also know you want a clear and comfortable exit-strategy. A stable investment exit plan is assured based on your preferred entry and exit threshold.
